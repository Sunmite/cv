Sir Shadow DeMatteis

High Flame Knight of the Realm

Sir Shadow is an honorable warrior, personally knighted by the High King. He earned that honor as a young man of just 16 years of age. After a long day of training, Sir Shadow was walking back to his family's home and saw a burning orphanage. He couragously ran into the inferno to help. After what seemed an enternity, the honorable Shadow rescued 2 dozen small children. The Queen herself, gave him the Flame Knight honorific.
